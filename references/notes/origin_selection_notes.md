# Origin Selection Notes

## Key principle
The correct origin is not any random pivot. It is the major pivot that begins the first meaningful impulse on the chosen timeframe.

## What to test
- ZigZag-based structural pivots
- fractal pivots
- ATR-threshold pivots
- range-expansion pivots
- longer-history major swing pivots

## Validation concept
A strong origin candidate should generate later geometry that aligns with future turning points more consistently than weaker candidates.

## Rule
Do not hard-code one origin method too early. Treat origin selection as a research problem.
